Class info

Location of Origin Stuttgart, Germany
Role Support

The Medic more than makes up for his lack of empathy for the sick, regard for human dignity, and any kind of verified professional training in medicine with an endless supply of enormous needles and a shivering eagerness for sticking them into exposed flesh. The Medic, who was raised in Stuttgart, Germany during a time when the Hippocratic oath was only an optional suggestion, saw healing as a typically unintentional byproduct of satiating his own morbid curiosity.
