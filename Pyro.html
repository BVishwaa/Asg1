The mysterious Pyro is only known to do two things: set things on fire, and remain silent. Only the part about lighting things on fire is indisputable, though. His occasionally rasping wheeze, according to others, may have been an attempt to speak through a mouth blocked by a filter and connected to lungs constantly exposed to his asbestos-lined suit. If he is even a man, he is a terrifying, baffling, on-fire Frankenstein of a man.
