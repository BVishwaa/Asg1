Location of Origin Boston, Massachusetts, USA
Role Offense

The Scout, the youngest of eight boys from the south side of Boston, grew up learning how to use his fists to solve problems. The Scout learned to escape because, with seven larger brothers at his side, conflicts frequently ended before the smallest of the litter could get close enough to punch. He sprinted nonstop till he could enter the battle ahead of his pack of crazy canine siblings.
