Class info

Location of Origin Ullapool, Scotland, United Kingdom
Role Defense

The six-year-old Demoman lost his birth parents due to a bad temper, a love of all things explosive, and a wicked scheme to kill the Loch Ness Monster. The boy's bomb-making abilities significantly improved later, back at the Crypt Grammar School for Orphans in the Scottish Highlands, close to Ullapool. However, his personality and total number of functional eyes did not.
